About the Author

Holly Jackson is a contemporary British author known for her young adult fiction, particularly in the mystery and thriller genres. "A Good Girl's Guide to Murder": This debut novel, published in 2019, follows a high school student investigating a murder case from five years prior. It has received critical acclaim and has won several awards. The success of her debut led to a sequel,"Good Girl, Bad Blood," released in 2020, which continues the story of the protagonist, Pip. Jackson's writing is characterized by its engaging narrative, well-developed characters, and intricate plotting. She often incorporates elements of suspense and social issues relevant to young adults. Holly Jackson studied creative writing at university, which has influenced her storytelling approach. She has expressed a passion for writing from a young age. Her works have been nominated for and won various literary awards, establishing her as a prominent voice in young adult literature. Jackson continues to write and is involved in various literary events and discussions, contributing to the young adult literary community.
